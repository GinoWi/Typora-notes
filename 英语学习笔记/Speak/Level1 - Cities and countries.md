# Level1 - Cities and countries

### 如何询问某人来自哪里

Q: Where are you from?  A: I'm from Shanghai, China. （这里如果具体描述某个城市，某个国家，可以在城市后面加`,`并在后面加上国家，代表某个国家的某个城市）

Q:Where is Lyon? A: Lyon is in New York.



### 表达某人出生在某地

I'm from Canada.

She's from China.

He's from the UK.



### 表达某人居住在某地（不一定出生在那里）

I live in China.

She lives in New York.

He lives in London, England.



### 询问一座城市或者一个乡村的状况

Q: What's it like? A: It's ...

Q: How's London? A: It's ...



### 课堂笔记：

My city has a park.

**Describe the park:**

There is a quite park.

It's very beautiful and big.

I usually run in the park every morning.

On the weekend/On weekends, it will be more crowded.

There are many kinds of flowers in the park.

Sometimes my family has a picnic in the park.（这里使用三单，因为family是group）

（如果这里不会描述野餐，可以使用活动进行代替，eg: Sometimes my family brings food and eat them in the park.）



Q: Where do you live?

A: I live in Shanghai.



Q: What's it like?

A: It's much/a lot bigger than my hometown. There are many skyscrapers in Shanghai, and it's near the sea. There are many museums and galleries in downtown/in the downtown area.

(downtown本身已经很特殊了）

I usually go to the downtown by subway because it's far from （where I live now / my home / my place）.

These days the weather is cloudy. / The weather is cloudy these days.

People are from different provinces.

It is in the south of China.



Q: Is it expensive to live there?

A: Yes, it is. There are many expensive restaurants and shops in the city.



Lifestyle:

Sometimes I go to the talk show on the weekend.

这里不使用 join 更像是参加。

Sometimes, we can meet some stars / celebrity / famous people.

这里使用 meet 可能会更加的确定一点，很多时候都是偶遇 run into celebrities。
